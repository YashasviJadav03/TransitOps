# Sprint 5: Fuel and Expense Tracking

## Objective
Implement financial tracking capabilities to monitor operational costs associated with fleet management.

## Key Deliverables
- **Fuel Logging:** Create entry forms and database models to record fuel consumption, including liters, cost, date, and the associated vehicle.
- **General Expenses:** Implement functionality to log supplementary operational expenses, such as tolls.
- **Cost Aggregation:** Develop automated calculations to compute the total operational cost per vehicle by aggregating fuel and maintenance expenses.
