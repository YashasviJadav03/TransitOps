# Sprint 6: Dashboard and KPIs

## Objective
Provide a centralized, high-level overview of fleet operations through a real-time analytics dashboard.

## Key Deliverables
- **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):** Develop modular summary cards displaying metrics such as Active Vehicles, Available Vehicles, Vehicles in Maintenance, Active Trips, Pending Trips, Drivers On Duty, and Fleet Utilization percentage.
- **Data Filtering:** Implement basic filtering controls allowing users to parse data by vehicle type, status, and region.
- **Analytics Display:** Calculate and visualize core operational analytics, including Fuel Efficiency, overarching Fleet Utilization, and Operational Cost. (Vehicle ROI may be included if time permits).
